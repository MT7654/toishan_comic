# The Last Letter Prompts

Built with the built-in `image_gen` tool using black-and-white manga prompts with a subtle cream paper texture and vertical webtoon framing.

## Shared visual anchor

- Kai: 17-year-old Singaporean student, straight black hair, school uniform, backpack, smartphone, slim build.
- Chen Liang: Taishanese migrant father, early 20s in 1905, lean, poor but determined, simple cotton shirt and work trousers, ages gradually over later pages.
- Mei: recognisable from child to adult through hair ribbon and gentle expression.

## Pages

1. Present-day Singapore outside the Wong Clan Association, Kai bored during a school heritage visit, classmates chatting behind him, daytime tropical light.
2. Inside the quiet association hall, Kai discovering an old wooden box holding qiaopi letters, a clan seal, and a black-and-white photograph.
3. Taishan village in 1905, Chen Liang leaving for Nanyang while young Mei clutches his sleeve on the dock road.
4. Early Singapore near the Kallang River, Chen Liang working as a labourer-carpenter among migrants, timber, boats, and sawdust.
5. Singapore qiaopi remittance house, Chen Liang handing over coins and a handwritten letter while a clerk stamps it.
6. Taishan family home, older Mei beside letters tied in red string while her mother reads one aloud, empty doorway behind them.
7. Singapore in 1942, older Chen Liang hiding a half-written letter in a tense wartime street without graphic violence.
8. Wartime Taishan, adult Mei waiting at the doorway with old letters and no new letter on the table.
9. After the war, adult Mei opening a damaged final letter with the visible incomplete words “Tell her...”
10. Present-day association hall, Kai closing the wooden box with new respect while soft silhouettes of Chen Liang and Mei linger like memory.
